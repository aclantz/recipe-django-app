My name is Alex Lantz and I’m a web developer with a prior 15-year career working as a professional ballet dancer. The competitive world of ballet ingrains patience, focus, and attention to detail. So many years in that career allowed me to not only grow as an artist, but to understand how to work with different people in an intimate setting. Solving problems and creating with colleagues is what brought me to web development, as I enjoy working with people on small details to help build something together. Learning through Career Foundry’s web development boot camp has broadened my skills and allowed me to continue creating in a virtual space.
My experience includes:

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • JavaScript
  • React
  • Angular
  • Python
  • Django

I hope to find an environment to continue creating and to keep learning while I grow in this new field. To contact me, or to look at some of my work, please visit my portfolio.
